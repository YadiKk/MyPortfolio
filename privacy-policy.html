This Privacy Policy describes how Yadulla Mirzayev ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you play our mobile games.

1. Information We Collect

Game Data:

  • Game progress and save data
  • High scores and achievements
  • Game settings and preferences
  • In-game purchases (if applicable)

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Game version and crash reports
  • Performance metrics

Analytics Data:

  • Gameplay statistics (anonymized)
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Technical performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our games
  • To save your game progress
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To analyze game performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored securely on your device and our servers
  • We use encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services: For achievements and leaderboards
  • Unity Analytics: For game analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Play Services: For app updates and billing

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (where possible)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: yadullamirzeyev5@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
